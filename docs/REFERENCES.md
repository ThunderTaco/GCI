### Twistronics

**Naaman, R., & Waldeck, D. H.** (2012). "Chiral-Induced Spin Selectivity Effect". *Journal of Physical Chemistry Letters* 3: 2178-2187.

**Medina, E., et al.** (2012). "Chiral Molecular Films as Electron Polarizers and Polarization Modulators". *EPL* 99: 17006.
